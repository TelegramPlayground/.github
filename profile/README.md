# 👋 Welcome to Telegram Playground

**TL;DR:** We build, tweak, and maintain the tools that make Telegram fun to play with. 

Ever found an amazing open-source project only to realize the original author ghosted it three years ago? Yeah, we hate that too. Telegram Playground was born out of necessity—we fork, maintain, and heavily customize Telegram libraries and bots to suit modern needs. Because sometimes, if you want something kept alive and kicking, you just have to do it yourself. ☕️

---

## 🛠️ What We're Building

We have quite a few repos, so we curated them into a few distinct vibes to help you find what you need:

### ⚡️ Core Frameworks & Forks
Keeping the heavy hitters updated and functional.
* [**pyroTGfork**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/pyroTGfork) — Our active fork of Pyrogram. 
* [**pyrogram-documentation**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/pyrogram) & [**pyrogram-tgcrypto**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/pyrogram-tgcrypto)
* [**Telethon**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/Telethon) — Tweaks for the popular asyncio Telegram client.
* [**build-tdlib**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/build-tdlib) & [**webogram**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/webogram)

### 🏗️ API & Schema Tools
Everything you need to track and parse Telegram's TL schemas.
* [**TG-APIs**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/TG-APIs)
* [**schema.tl**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/schema.tl)
* [**tl-differ**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/tl-differ) & [**tl-to-json**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/tl-to-json)

### 🎬 Bots, Media Indexing & Fun Stuff
Automations, reactions, and massive movie/series indexing tools.
* [**IMDb-Telegram-BOT**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/IMDb-Telegram-BOT) & [**Free-Movie-Series-DB-API**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/Free-Movie-Series-DB-API)
* [**Search-SUBtitles**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/Search-SUBtitles)
* [**BotIndexUserBot**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/BotIndexUserBot) & [**ReactionAutoBot**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/ReactionAutoBot)

### 🧰 Utilities & Micro-projects
From speed testing to media parsing, plus a sandbox of experimental bots.
* [**bmt**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/bmt) — Telegram Clients Speed Test.
* [**youtube-search-python**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/youtube-search-python) & [**parse-torrent-name**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/parse-torrent-name)
* [**wa-tg-bridge**](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/wa-tg-bridge) — WhatsApp to Telegram bridge.
* **The Sandbox:** [OtherBots](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/OtherBots), [bg](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/bg), [wj](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/wj), [tk](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/tk), [jw](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/jw), [bw](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/bw), [io](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/io), [fpCt](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/fpCt), [shashi](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/shashi), [itnwj](https://github.com/TelegramPlayground/itnwj).
